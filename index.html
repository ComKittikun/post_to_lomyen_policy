Privacy Policy for post_to_lomyen_cafe Effective Date: [24 Apr 2025] Thank you for using post_to_lomyen_cafe, an automation application developed to assist with posting content to the Lomyen Cafe&Bar Facebook page. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our services. 1. Information We Collect To provide our services, our application accesses: Google Sheets: To read structured content such as captions, schedules, or post metadata. Google Drive: To retrieve image files or other media for use in social media posts. We only access the specific files or folders that you explicitly grant access to via Google OAuth. 2. How We Use Your Information The data obtained through Google APIs is used solely to: Automate the posting of content from your specified Google Sheets and Google Drive to the Lomyen Cafe&Bar Facebook page. We do not use your information for any other purposes. 3. Data Storage We do not store any user data permanently on our servers. Any access tokens or temporary data are securely stored and only for the duration necessary to complete the posting process. 4. Data Sharing We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to third parties. Your Google account information and files remain private and are only accessed with your consent. 5. User Control and Consent You can revoke access to your Google data at any time via your Google Account Permissions. 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users via the application interface. 7.
